About 404 Books

An oasis for book lovers, dreamers, and learners.

KNOW US BETTER

Welcome to 404 BOOKS, your premier gateway for literary adventure in this digital age.

404 BOOKS established in April 2025, located in Malaysia’s first fully integrated green township of Sunway City.
Through our web platform, we've help lots of readers to find their ideal books. We've put our heart and soul into creating a collection of books that spans genres to ensuring that every reader will discover their next great read.

OUR MISSION

To make quality literature accessible to everyone, everywhere, while fostering a vibrant community of book lovers through our digital platform.

OUR VISION

To become the most trusted and beloved online bookstore in Malaysia, where every reader finds their perfect literary companion.
